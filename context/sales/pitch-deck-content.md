## Deck Usage Guidelines

### Enterprise Deck
- Present to VP Marketing, Creative Directors, Brand Managers
- Allow 45 minutes (30 presentation, 15 discussion)
- Always customize Slide 8 (Results) with category-relevant examples
- Have governance documentation ready for legal follow-up
- Send ROI calculator as follow-up within 24 hours

### Emerging Brand Deck
- Present to Founders, Marketing Leads
- Allow 20-25 minutes (15 presentation, 10 discussion)
- Lead with Before/After if possible -- visuals sell faster than data
- Emphasize the free sample offer -- lower the barrier to try
- Follow up with sample images within 48 hours of meeting

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not lead with AI technology -- lead with the business problem
- Do not compare to stock photography -- compare to traditional production
- Do not skip compliance slides for enterprise -- it's their top concern
- Do not use the enterprise deck for emerging brands -- it's too long and too formal
- Do not promise specific conversion lift numbers without qualifying them as category averages
