# LinkedIn Post Skill

Generate LinkedIn posts that follow brand voice, messaging framework, and LinkedIn playbook.

## When to Use

When Content Writer needs to create LinkedIn content based on a brief.

## Prerequisites

- Brand guidelines exist (`/context/marketing/brand-guidelines.md`)
- Tone of voice defined (`/context/marketing/tone-of-voice.md`)
- Messaging framework exists (`/context/marketing/messaging-framework.md`)
- LinkedIn playbook exists (`/context/marketing/playbooks/linkedin-playbook.md`)

## Process

1. Load context: brand-guidelines.md, tone-of-voice.md, messaging-framework.md, linkedin-playbook.md
2. Identify content pillar and funnel stage from brief
3. Select post format from playbook (see templates below)
4. Draft post following playbook structure
5. Apply tone modifiers for LinkedIn channel
6. Self-review against checklist
7. Save to `/outputs/content/linkedin/`

## Post Templates

### Problem-Agitation-Solution
Hook with a specific pain point → Describe what happens when it goes unsolved → Introduce the solution angle → CTA

### Story-Lesson-CTA
Personal or industry story (2-3 sentences) → Key takeaway → Bridge to business insight → CTA

### Contrarian Take
Bold statement that challenges conventional wisdom → Evidence or reasoning → Reframe → Invite discussion

### Industry Data + Insight
Share a specific stat or data point → Provide context others miss → Draw non-obvious conclusion → Ask for reactions

### Behind-the-Scenes
Show a real process or decision → What you learned → Why it matters to the audience → Engagement prompt

### Customer Result Spotlight
Specific outcome (quantified if possible) → How it was achieved → Why it matters → CTA

### Question/Poll
Open with a polarizing or thought-provoking question → Provide 2-3 options → Share your perspective → Invite responses

## Self-Review Checklist

- [ ] Hook in first line (before "see more" cutoff)
- [ ] Aligns with brand voice
- [ ] Maps to a content pillar
- [ ] 150-300 words
- [ ] Clear CTA or engagement prompt at end
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] No jargon without context
- [ ] Would the target persona stop scrolling for this?
